Common questions

How often should I replace my brake pads?

Typically every 30,000-50,000 miles, but it depends on driving habits. We'll inspect them during your visit.

What are signs of brake problems?

Squealing, grinding, vibration, or a soft brake pedal. Bring your car in if you notice any of these.
